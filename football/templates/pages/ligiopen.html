LigiOpen

Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the support and funding of football in Kenya to create employment opportunities for the youths in Kenya.

Vision

Our vision is to see Kenyan football having the same reception as the UK football. More entrepreneurship opportunities for the youth and a solid Kenyan football fanbase.

Funding

Our source of funding is through donations, ticket sales and sponsorship opportunities. We are open about our financial operations and we will always account for the funds upto the last coin.
